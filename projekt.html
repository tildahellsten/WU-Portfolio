About +

My app, Aurora, is designed to offer young people an intuitive, AI-driven way to manage mental health challenges. Recognizing the need for alternatives to medication, especially for those looking to handle anxiety and stress more independently, the app focuses on proactive and personalized mental wellness support. Users can connect their heart rate monitor for real-time insights, allowing the AI to predict and address anxiety before it escalates, while also offering exercises and a journal for tracking well-being over time. In developing this app, I focused on creating a user-friendly experience, tailored primarily for young women aged 15–25, where easy navigation and targeted tools, like guided breathing exercises and a responsive AI chat, encourage consistent use. My goal is to empower users to build healthy coping mechanisms and reduce dependence on medication, using technology to improve self-awareness and resilience in mental health.
